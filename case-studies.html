Our Impact in Action

Learn how ClimaFocus has helped organizations worldwide navigate climate challenges with data-driven solutions.

Northern Norway Parks

Challenge: Understanding and mitigating climate impacts (permafrost thaw, changing river flows) in sensitive park ecosystems.

Approach: We met with park rangers to define key climate metrics, provided detailed projections up to 2100, and outlined possible mitigation strategies.

Outcome: The parks implemented river management measures that helped protect ecosystems and reduce erosion risks.

Ocean Conservancy Memo

Challenge: Providing a concise yet comprehensive briefing on climate change in the Central Arctic Ocean.

Approach: Conducted a rapid literature review, synthesized the latest Arctic warming data, and delivered a policy-ready memo.

Outcome: Ocean Conservancy integrated these insights into advocacy efforts, influencing policy discussions and public awareness campaigns.
