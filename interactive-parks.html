Interactive Data Visualization for Priority Climate Risks

Reisa National Park Explorer

This tool provides an overview of potential climate futures for Reisa National Park. It’s oriented around the park’s main conservation priorities—like forest fires or river access. Select a priority from the dropdown menu to see its climate drivers, classified by a “traffic light” system (safe, neutral, dangerous).

Adjust the time window and emissions scenario to explore changes over time. Clicking on the map reveals local climate drivers (300 m resolution). See how these metrics shift under different scenarios and plan accordingly.
