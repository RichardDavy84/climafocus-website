Who We Are

ClimaFocus is a climate consultancy dedicated to delivering data-driven insights and guidance to government agencies, private companies, NGOs, and more. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge climate research and real-world decision-making.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Richard Davy

  • Member of the Norwegian Scientific Academy for Polar Research
  • 15+ years of postdoctoral experience in climate science
  • 45+ peer-reviewed publications, over 2,300 citations
  • Lead/Principal Investigator on multiple large-scale climate research grants

“After years in front-line research—developing advanced climate models and exploring future projections—I recognized a growing need for actionable climate intelligence. ClimaFocus was born from the idea that the best data and insights should be accessible, understandable, and directly applicable for diverse stakeholders.”
