About Climate Compass

Bridging Cutting-Edge Climate Science and Real-World Decision Making

Founder

Dr. Richard Davy

  • 15+ years postdoctoral experience in climate science
  • 45+ peer-reviewed publications, 2,300+ citations
  • Lead investigator on many National and European research grants
  • Member of the Norwegian Scientific Academy for Polar Research

After extensive research in climate modeling and data analysis, Dr. Davy recognized the need to translate cutting-edge science into accessible, practical strategies. Climate Compass was founded to connect organizations with the data and insights they need to adapt, innovate, and lead on climate action.

Interested in staying updated? Follow Dr. Davy on social media for the latest climate insights and project news!
