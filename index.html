Welcome to Climate Compass

We offer world-class climate consulting, data-driven solutions, and expert guidance for businesses, governments, and NGOs. Our team brings deep scientific expertise, providing high-resolution climate datasets, tailored analyses, and strategic advice.

1) Climate Data

Bringing climate data and uncertainty into focus for local issues. Many organizations have a qualitative grasp of climate change but lack the **quantitative** insights needed to prioritize and adapt effectively. We solve this by delivering **high-resolution**, uncertainty-aware data and analyses directly relevant to your local or regional context.

Why Our Solution?
We take raw model outputs and convert them into **decision-ready** datasets—removing biases, refining resolution, and highlighting uncertainties. This empowers you to plan for a range of scenarios, ensuring you’re not caught off-guard by evolving climate realities.

2) Climate Consulting

We provide **comprehensive, comprehensible** summaries of the latest climate research. If you feel lost in an ocean of scientific literature—where IPCC reports may be too dense or not tailored to your local needs—our personalized consulting offers the clarity and context you require.

Why Our Solution?
Our team distills **cutting-edge** climate science into **actionable guidance**, bridging the gap between global reports and **region-specific** challenges. We equip you with accessible briefs, clear communication, and tailor-made recommendations.

3) Interactive Data

Bring your climate data to life with **web-based tools** that let you explore scenarios and time horizons dynamically. As your data needs evolve, these platforms help you **visualize**, **compare**, and **adapt** in real time.

The Problem We Solve:
Many clients find static reports limiting because their questions shift over time. Our interactive tools give you **ongoing flexibility**, ensuring you can delve deeper into the data, test what-if scenarios, and keep pace with changing challenges—without needing to commission a brand-new study every time.

Curious about real-world applications? Head over to our Case Studies page for examples. We also offer **training courses** on how to use these tools effectively in your organization.
